Vacancy Information

Company: Financial Solutions

Position:IT Security Specialist

Location: Dublin

Salary:€60,000

Required experience: 5 years

Status: Open


Job Description:

Reporting to the IT Services Operations Manager the IT Security Specialist will be responsible for the organisation’s technical security landscape, both physical and digital and will oversee and co-ordinate security efforts across CRM business. This role will help identify security initiatives and standards and participate closely in related areas such as business continuity planning, fraud prevention, risk assessment and privacy. He/she will work closely with various groups across the organisation to understand corporate requirements and develop and implement a security strategy in line with these requirements, he/she will need to interact with project teams to help review designs to ensure these designs are compatible with security standards. There will be on-going engagement with both internal and external audit teams to help validate and improve our security standards Person Spec and Competencies (Please read in conjunction with the Key Result Areas overleaf) Essential Qualifications, Training and Experience: * Educated to at least degree standard, higher degree preferred * A minimum of 2 years in a security-related role * An excellent and articulate communicator who has the ability to communicate security-related concepts to a broad range of technical and non-technical stakeholders * Existing certifications according to CISSP or CISSA would be beneficial * Good knowledge of encryption technologies and their concepts * Knowledge of office networks, Web proxy devices, DLP, SIEM (Splunk), MetaSploit, IDS/IPS Systems * Knowledge of the following technical platforms - UNIX / Linux, Windows, Oracle, Python, Routers, IP Phone systems * Working experience with regulations, audit and compliance frameworks (e.g. PCI-DSS, ISO, etc.).
